Religious/Non-Religious?

Type of Event:

Statutory Holiday

Where in Canada Observed:

Observed across Canada. Schools and most businesses are closed.

Reasons:

This holiday marks the change from summer vacation to the beginning of a new school year in September.

What do people do?

Many families with children return from holidays, and a new back-to-school seriousness sets in for September. People usually relax, have last-opportunity summer parties, and prepare for colder months of Fall and Winter season.
